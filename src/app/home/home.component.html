LOGIN INSTRUCTION

Instruction

To log in to the system, click the Login button.
Enter your username in the Username field, and enter your password in the Password field. After logging in to the system, you will be redirected to a page with a list of competitions assigned to you for judging. When you select a contest, you will be redirected to the entry page. After filling in the table for entering grades, click the Save button.
Best of luck!
